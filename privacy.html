Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

FYNNECT Wealth Advisors ("FYNNECT", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through fynnect.com and our advisory services, and how we use and protect it.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you share with us — such as your name, phone number, and email — when you book a consultation or contact us.
  • Financial planning information you voluntarily provide during consultations, such as income, goals, and existing investments, used solely to prepare your financial plan.
  • Basic usage data collected by our hosting provider (such as IP address and browser type) in standard server logs.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide financial planning and advisory services.
  • To schedule and manage consultations, including through our partner platform, Wealthy (BuildWealth Technologies), whose own privacy policy applies to interactions on their platform.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data protection

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to keep your information secure and retain it only as long as needed to serve you or as required by law.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at deepak@fynnect.com.
